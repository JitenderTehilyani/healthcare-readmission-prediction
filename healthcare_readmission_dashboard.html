Model Conclusion
Balanced Logistic Regression was selected because healthcare prediction prioritizes recall over overall accuracy.

The model successfully identified 40.7% of patients likely to be readmitted within 30 days — far superior to standard models that achieve near-zero recall.

In clinical settings, catching 40.7% of at-risk patients enables timely intervention, reducing costs and improving patient outcomes.
